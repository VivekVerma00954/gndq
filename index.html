Our Mission

To establish Queenstown's first Gurudwara Sahib, providing a sacred space for prayer, community gathering, and spiritual growth.

Our Vision

A fully established Gurudwara Sahib serving local Sikhs, visitors, and the wider community through Seva, Langar, and Sangat.

Langar Seva

Free community kitchen serving nutritious meals to all, regardless of faith, background, or status – a core Sikh principle.

Community Hub

Bringing together the Sikh and wider community through religious celebrations and cultural events.

☬ Sri Guru Granth Sahib Ji

We follow the eternal Guru – Sri Guru Granth Sahib Ji – and the teachings of the Ten Gurus as our guiding light.

🕊️ Sikh Principles

Seva (selfless service), Simran (meditation), Sangat (community), and Langar (equality) form our foundation.

🏔️ Queenstown's First Gurudwara Sahib

Currently in development phase – working with NZ government and community to establish a permanent sacred space.
